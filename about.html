Our Story

Celebrating India's rich textile heritage through contemporary designs

Welcome to Fashion Store

Founded in 2015, Fashion Store is a celebration of India's diverse textile traditions. We bring together the finest handlooms and craftsmanship from across the country, offering you authentic ethnic wear that blends tradition with contemporary aesthetics.

Our journey began with a simple mission - to make traditional Indian clothing accessible to the modern consumer without compromising on quality or authenticity. Today, we work directly with over 200 weavers and artisans from Varanasi, Kanchipuram, Chanderi, Jaipur, and other textile hubs of India.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of the skilled hands that wove it, the traditional techniques that shaped it, and the cultural heritage it represents. We take pride in preserving these ancient crafts while adapting them to suit today's fashion sensibilities.
