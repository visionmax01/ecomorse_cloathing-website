Product Description

This Banarasi silk saree is a masterpiece of Indian craftsmanship. Woven with pure silk threads and adorned with intricate zari work, it showcases the rich heritage of Varanasi's weaving tradition. The saree features traditional motifs inspired by Mughal architecture, with a rich red base and golden borders.

The fabric has a luxurious drape and sheen that enhances the wearer's elegance. The pallu is particularly ornate, with dense zari patterns that catch the light beautifully. The saree comes with a matching blouse piece that complements the main design.

Care Instructions

  • Dry clean only to preserve the zari work
  • Store in a cool, dry place wrapped in muslin cloth
  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent color fading
  • Do not iron directly on zari work
